Chicken Biryani

Description

Chicken biryani has many variations, and this recipe focuses on one of the easiest and most flavorful methods to prepare it at home.
The preparation time for this dish is approximately 30 minutes, while the cooking process takes about 1 hour and 20 minutes, resulting
in a total time of 1 hour and 50 minutes. This recipe yields enough food to serve up to 6 people, making it suitable for family meals or
small gatherings. It includes a complete list of ingredients needed to achieve a rich aroma and taste, along with clear, step-by-step
instructions that guide you from start to finish. By carefully following this recipe, even beginners can successfully prepare a delicious
and satisfying chicken biryani.

Ingredients

  • 4 small potatoes, peeled and halved
  • 2 large onions, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon minced fresh ginger root
  • 2 medium tomatoes, peeled and chopped
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • ½ teaspoon chili powder
  • ½ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • ½ teaspoon ground turmeric
  • 2 tablespoons plain yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh mint leaves
  • ½ teaspoon ground cardamom
  • 1 (2 inch) piece cinnamon stick
  • 3 pounds boneless, skinless chicken pieces cut into chunks
